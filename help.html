üöÄ Quick Start Guide

Step 1: Join a Meeting

Navigate to a supported meeting platform (Google Meet, Zoom, or Microsoft Teams) and join a meeting.

Step 2: Open the Extension

Click the Meeting Translator extension icon in your browser toolbar to open the control popup.

Step 3: Start Recording

Click the "Start Recording" button to begin real-time transcription. The extension will automatically detect the meeting video and start processing audio.

Step 4: View Transcripts

Click "Show Interface" to see the live transcript overlay. You can drag and resize it as needed.

Step 5: Export Results

When finished, use the "Export" button to save your transcripts to a file.

üîß Troubleshooting

Extension not working?

  • Make sure you're on a supported meeting platform
  • Check that microphone permissions are granted
  • Refresh the page and try again
  • Ensure Chrome is up to date

No audio detected?

  • Verify that the meeting has active audio/video
  • Check browser audio permissions
  • Try adjusting audio quality settings

Poor transcription quality?

  • Ensure good audio quality in the meeting
  • Check speech recognition language settings
  • Adjust confidence threshold in settings

üîê Privacy & Security

Meeting Translator processes audio locally in your browser using the Web Speech API. No audio data is sent to external servers. Transcripts are stored locally and can be exported or cleared at any time.
